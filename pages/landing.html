About The

PROJECT

The Pathways Project invites aspiring footballers aged 15-22, with a focus on professional academy players. Embracing inclusivity, we collaborate with both male and female candidates, envisioning a world where your game propels you beyond the field, unlocking new opportunities.

Our mission is to provide a tailored framework for you, addressing personal career uncertainties and challenges post-football, whether it's feeling low or not knowing where to start. The project empowers players, guiding them through seamless career transitions.

Recognizing the preference for individuals with athletic backgrounds, we step in to ensure your skills shine beyond the field. So, get ready, because at the Pathways Project, your journey continues.

our

Working together with clubs, current and ex-players and industry we aim to improve the number of ex-players transitioning into careers.
